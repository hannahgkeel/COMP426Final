Monterrey's Covid-19 Initiatives

Monterrey's recognizes the necessity for extra safety precautions to protect it's customers and employees. Per Governor Roy Cooper: "Restaurants remain subject to capacity limits and other requirements for in-person dining. Face coverings are still required in public when it is not possible to maintain social distancing from non-household members. In Phase 3, this requirement applies to any public place or business - indoor or outdoor. Alcohol sales are required to cease from 11 p.m. to 7 a.m." Additionally, Monterrey requires each guest to wear a face mask unless they are seated at their table. This includes if the guest goes to the bathroom. Monterrey prohibits parties greater than 6 people. If you have a larger party you must sit at different tables and enter the waitlist system separately. If you do not have a smart phone to enter the waitlist, please see the hostess inside for assistance.
